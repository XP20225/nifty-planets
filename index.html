Planet Status Badges

    

      ℞Retrograde — planet moving backward against the zodiac
      ☀Combust — within combustion orb of Sun, weakened (Mercury: 14° direct / 12° retrograde; Venus: 10° direct / 16° retrograde)
      ⊙Cazimi — within 1° of Sun's centre; "in the heart of the Sun", extremely powerful (opposite of combust)
      ☽EEclipse zone — Rahu or Ketu within 18° of Sun; solar eclipse conditions possible (not combustion)
      ExExalted — planet in its sign of maximum strength
      DeDebilitated — planet in its sign of minimum strength
      ⌂Own sign — planet in a sign it rules
      VVargottama — same sign in both Rashi (D1) and Navamsha (D9), very strong
      GGandanta — within 3°20' of a water-fire sign junction (Pis/Ari, Can/Leo, Sco/Sag), sensitive degree
      AKAtmakaraka — planet with highest degree in its sign; represents the soul's primary drive
      ⚔Graha Yuddha (Planetary War) — two planets within 1° of each other in the same sign (Me, Ve, Ma, Ju, Sa only)
